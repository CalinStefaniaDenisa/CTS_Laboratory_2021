Title of the bachelor topic: 
	Mood-driven travel web application


Description of the problem to be solved by DECORATOR pattern:
	Changing the characteristics of some vacation idea at run-time
	- During a user's searching depending on the number of ratings provided, some vacation ideas have extra discounts or are free
	- The details can change dynamically depending on the user's interaction with the website
		

Advantages for the solution:
	- expansion of function of classes without inheritance
	- resource-optimized functionalities


Disadvantages:
	- high number of objects
	- difficult debugging process
